Product Description:


Stay stylish this season with this beautiful green pattern Chiffon Scarf from "Ritzy". A hijab essential for any wardrobe, this lightweight and versatile scarf is sure to help you stand out from the crowd. Crafted from high-quality materials, this timeless accessory is perfect for any occasion.

Dimensions: x 65cm.


Garment Care:

  • Fill your sink with cold water.
  • Use delicate detergent (no bleach).
  • Don't rub piecees of the scarf against each other, this way damage the fabric
  • Leave the scarf in water for 10 minutes.
  • Rinse with water.
  • Only air dry (place your scarf between 2 towels to remove excess water).
  • Iron inside out on medium or low heat.
